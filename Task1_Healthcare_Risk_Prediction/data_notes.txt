Dataset: Synthetic Hospital Readmission Data

Columns:
- patient_id
- age
- gender
- blood_pressure
- glucose_level
- heart_rate
- past_visits
- days_since_last_visit
- readmission_within_30_days (target)

Preprocessing Steps:
1. Remove missing values and invalid records.
2. Normalize numeric columns.
3. Encode gender (0 = Male, 1 = Female).
4. Split into training and testing sets (80/20).
5. Store cleaned data in AWS S3.
